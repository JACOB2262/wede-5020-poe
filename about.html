Our Journey & Mission

Uplifting lives every day by providing quality goods at reduced prices.

Our History

Checkers was acquired by Shoprite in 1991. In 2001, we successfully repositioned the brand to target the middle-to-upper income market, ensuring that world-class retail is accessible to all.

In 2016, we launched the FreshX flagship concept to provide a premium shopping experience that competes with the world's best retailers.

Our Vision & Goal

Our primary goal is to be Africa"s most profitable retail market. We achieve this by focusing on:

  • Reducing prices for our customers without compromising quality.
  • Providing energy-restoring, high-quality food and goods.
  • Serving a diverse audience of both young and old customers.
